Player Types

  • HLS Player (player.html): Standard player for .m3u8 streams using HLS.js

  • Iframe Player (iframe-player.html): Embeds external player pages (like sportsonline.cv) in an iframe
Important Notes:
- HLS Players work with .m3u8 stream URLs only
- Iframe Player works with external player pages (PHP/HTML pages with embedded players)
- CORS restrictions may block some HLS streams - try the iframe player as alternative
- All players display FOOTHOLICS.IN branding
