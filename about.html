About Foot Holics

Built by fans, for fans — your home for football coverage worldwide

⚽

Live. Passion. Football.

Foot Holics is a fan-driven football platform founded with a single purpose: to give every football fan around the world access to live match coverage, real-time standings, upcoming fixtures, and the latest news from all the leagues they love.

We are not affiliated with any club, league, or broadcaster. We are supporters, just like you. This platform was born out of passion for the beautiful game.

What We Offer

Football News

Daily football news covering transfers, match previews, post-match analysis, injury updates and more — sourced and aggregated from the world's top sports outlets.

League Standings

Up-to-date tables for all major European leagues. Track the title race, European qualification spots and relegation battles in real time.

Upcoming Fixtures

See every scheduled match for the next 14 days across the Premier League, La Liga, Bundesliga, Serie A, Ligue 1 and the Champions League.

Live Match Coverage

Aggregated streaming links for live matches. We curate public third-party sources so fans never have to search multiple sites to find a stream.

Community Channels

Join tens of thousands of fans on our WhatsApp and Telegram channels for instant match alerts, stream links and post-match discussions.

Mobile Friendly

Foot Holics is designed to work perfectly on any screen — phone, tablet or desktop. Watch matches wherever you are, whenever you want.

Our Approach

Foot Holics was started because we believe football coverage should be accessible to everyone, regardless of subscription costs or geographic restrictions. We aggregate publicly available content and provide a clean, fast, ad-light experience.

All match previews and editorial content published on this site is original, written with care by our editorial team. News headlines are sourced from and attributed to original outlets. Streaming links are aggregated from public third-party sources and are not hosted by us.

If you are a rights holder with a concern, please visit our DMCA page or email us at footholicsin@gmail.com.
