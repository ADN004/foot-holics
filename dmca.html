DMCA / Copyright Policy

Last Updated: October 27, 2025

Important Notice: Foot Holics does NOT host, upload, or store any video content. We are a link aggregator that directs users to publicly available third-party sources.

1. Our Role

We aggregate and display links to streaming content hosted on third-party websites. We have no control over the content, quality, or availability of these external streams.

2. Copyright Holders

If you believe your copyrighted content is being streamed on a site we link to:

  • Contact the hosting website directly - they control the content
  • Send us a takedown request and we will remove the link
  • We respond to valid DMCA takedown requests within 24-48 hours

3. DMCA Takedown Request

To file a valid takedown request, provide:

  • Your contact information (name, email, phone)
  • Description of the copyrighted work
  • URL of the link on our site
  • Statement of good faith belief
  • Statement that the information is accurate
  • Physical or electronic signature

DMCA Contact:

Email: footholicsin@gmail.com

Subject Line: "DMCA Takedown Request"

4. Response Time

We typically respond to valid requests within:

  • Urgent cases: 24-48 hours
  • Standard cases: 5-7 business days

5. Counter-Notification

If you believe content was removed in error, you may file a counter-notification. We will forward it to the original complainant.

6. Repeat Infringers

We will remove links from sources that repeatedly infringe copyrights.

7. No Liability

We are not responsible for copyright violations by third-party hosting sites. All legal actions should be directed at content hosts, not link aggregators.
