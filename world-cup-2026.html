How It Works

Tournament Format

For the first time ever, 48 nations compete across three host countries in a historic expanded tournament

Group Stage
48 teams in 12 groups of 4. June 11 – July 3.

Top 2 from each group + 8 best third-placed teams = 32 teams advance.
Knockout Rounds
Round of 32 → Round of 16 → Quarterfinals → Semifinals. July 4 – 14.

Pure elimination — one loss ends the dream.
The Final
July 19, 2026  ·  MetLife Stadium, New Jersey.

The biggest stage in football — watched by over 5 billion people worldwide.
